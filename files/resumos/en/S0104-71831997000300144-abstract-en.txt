Abstract The following paper comments the intellectual production of Marcel Mauss in his work reception in Brazil and points out the difficulties of a whole vision because of his work&#8217;s dispersion and fragmentation. It presents the trials of Mauss&#8217; disciples to unify and order the Master&#8217;s writings throw commented issues. It does a translations&#8217; inventory of the author in Portuguese language, showing it&#8217;s virtuouness and gaps. Finally it presents a periodization and a chronology of Mauss&#8217; issues in France and in Brazil.
