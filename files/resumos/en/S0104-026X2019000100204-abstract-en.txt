Abstract: The article presents the results of a research done with delegates who work in the Women's Police stations for Assistance to Women in 19 regions of Santa Catarina. The main objectives were to understand the profile of the delegates, to analyze the adequacy of the police activities and the administrative structural conditions of the Police Stations in relation to the Technical Norm of Standardization of the Women's Police Station for Assistance to Women. The methodological approach is based on quantitative data obtained through the use of an online questionnaire answered by delegates. The results of the research indicate that the Police Stations are mostly commanded by men delegates, that structural conditions and the activities carried out in Police Station units fall short of the provisions of the Technical Norm.
