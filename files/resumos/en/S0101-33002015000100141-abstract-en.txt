ABSTRACTIn the following interview, Professor of Visual Arts Sônia Salzstein discusses the History of Art produced in Brazil, formalism and Social History of Art in this debate and changes in the scenario of Brazilian art institutions, from the perspective of her own experience as a lecturer and critic. This statement is part of the project &#8220;History and art criticism today: references, dialogues&#8221;, developed by the Núcleo de Pesquisa e Mediação do Centro Universitário Maria Antonia, da USP, coordinated by Ana Cândida Avelar.
