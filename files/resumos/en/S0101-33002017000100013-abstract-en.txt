ABSTRACT The purpose of this article, that introduces the dossier titled &#8220;Policy Transfer, Diffusion and Circulation&#8221;, is, firstly, to present a simple and synthetic map of the field of studies of international policy diffusion, in order to situate the reader from Brazil and Latin America, as well as others interested from different regions. In its second section, the article presents a survey of the state of research on policy diffusion that has been produced in Brazil.
